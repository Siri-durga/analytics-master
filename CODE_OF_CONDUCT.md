# Analytics Master Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to fostering a welcoming, respectful, and inclusive environment for everyone who contributes to this project. We encourage collaboration, knowledge sharing, and professional behavior.

## Our Standards

Examples of positive behavior include:

- Treating everyone with respect and professionalism.
- Being open to constructive feedback.
- Supporting and encouraging fellow contributors.
- Respecting different perspectives and experiences.
- Collaborating honestly and responsibly.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or abusive language.
- Personal attacks or offensive comments.
- Publishing another person's private information without permission.
- Any behavior that disrupts a respectful and professional environment.

## Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for ensuring that this Code of Conduct is followed. Any inappropriate behavior may result in warnings, removal of contributions, or restriction from participating in the project.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all interactions within this repository, including issues, pull requests, discussions, and other community spaces.

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please contact the project maintainer through GitHub.

## Enforcement

All reports will be reviewed fairly and confidentially. Appropriate action will be taken depending on the severity of the issue.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is inspired by the Contributor Covenant and adapted for the Analytics Master project.
